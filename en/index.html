Frequently Asked Questions

How is your control module different from ELRS or Crossfire?

Our control modules use two ranges for drone control simultaneously. Additionally, they implement pseudorandom hopping sequences (FHSS). This makes the control highly resistant to narrowband interference.

What are the advantages of your drone over bombers based on Mavic and Autel?

Our drone is primarily designed as a military bomber, allowing the user to adapt it for combat operations easily. It also has more resistant communication, digital video with channel switching capability to avoid jamming, and higher payload capacity for similar sizes.

What is included with the drone?

The drone comes with:
- External ground antenna station
- 50 m cable reel
- Ground control unit consists of a remote control, tablet, and all necessary hubs for efficient operation

How is the MK-19 turret controlled?

You can easily control the turret using either a tablet with the coordinates entered by a user or manually with a pre-loaded area map.

Does the turret size allow it to be transported in a pickup truck or other civilian vehicles?

Yes, the turret size (1200×1400×800 mm) and weight (47 kilograms) allow transportation in civilian vehicles or outlanders.
